Flash Instructions ESP32-S3 (MicroPython BLE MIDI)

1. Masuk mode download:
   - Tahan BOOT
   - Tekan RESET
   - Lepas BOOT

2. Flash firmware:
   esptool.py --chip esp32s3 write_flash -z 0x0 firmware/micropython.bin

3. Setelah flash:
   copy main.py, ble_midi.py, usb_midi.py ke filesystem internal.

4. Reset ESP32-S3.
